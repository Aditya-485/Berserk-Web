Characters

Guts

Born from a corpse, he became the Black Swordsman, wielding the massive Dragonslayer sword, seeking vengeance against Griffith.

Griffith

Leader of the Band of the Hawk, sacrificed comrades to become a demon king named Femto.

Casca

Warrior and Guts' love interest, mentally broken after the Eclipse.

Skull Knight

Mysterious warrior opposing the God Hand with a dimension-slicing sword.

Luca

Kind-hearted prostitute who helped Casca during chaos in Albion.

Flora

Witch and mentor to Schierke, sacrificed to protect others.

Zodd the Immortal

Ancient, bloodthirsty demon warrior who respects only strength.
